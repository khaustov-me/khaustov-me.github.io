Summary

I am an Artificial Intelligence enthusiast with a passion for exploring AI, machine learning, and innovation. During my Ph.D., I focused on machine learning for multi-agent systems, working to create “human-like” virtual agents for football (soccer).

Since October 2023, I have been working as the CTO/Head of AI at Eyes, Japan Co. Ltd. In this role, I develop and implement strategic technological plans for various projects, ensuring alignment with the overall business goals. I lead AI and machine learning initiatives, collaborating with research institutions such as the National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST), Kobe University, and the National Rehabilitation Center for Persons with Disabilities. I also manage and mentor a team of AI engineers and data scientists, fostering collaboration and skill development.

For the AI community, I have been an active volunteer coordinator at AAAI Conferences for about five years, overseeing and coordinating more than a hundred volunteers. I feel fortunate to work across different areas of machine learning and AI, constantly exploring new frontiers in this exciting field.
